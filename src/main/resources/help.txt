Use the following keys to move, attack, or open adjacent doors:

     y  k  u      7  8  9
     h     l      4     6
     b  j  n      1  2  3

You can also use the arrow keys, though diagonal movement 
is not possible.  The keys . or number pad 5 indicate to stay still.

    Key    Action
    ===    ========================
      .    Stay still
      <    Go upstairs
      >    Go downstairs
      ?    See help
      C    Character description
      W    Take off equipment
      b    Walk southwest
      c    Close door
      d    Drop an item
      f    Fire
      g    Get objects on the floor
      h    Walk west
      i    Inventory list
      j    Walk south
      k    Walk north
      l    Walk east
      n    Walk southeast
      q    Quaff a potion
      s    Save
      u    Walk northeast
      w    Wear/wield equipment
      x    Examine nearby things
      y    Walk northwest

Press any key to continue.
